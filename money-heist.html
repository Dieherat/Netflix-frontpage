**Money Heist (La Casa de Papel) - Season 1, Episode 1: "Efectuar lo acordado"**

The episode begins with Tokyo, a skilled robber on the run after a botched heist that resulted in her boyfriend’s death. Just as she’s about to be caught by the police, she is rescued by a mysterious man known as The Professor, who offers her a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity: to be part of a meticulously planned heist.

Tokyo is taken to a secluded house where she meets seven other criminals, each with their own unique skills and codenames based on cities:

  • Berlin – The group's leader inside the Royal Mint
  • Rio – A young hacker and Tokyo’s eventual love interest
  • Nairobi – A forger with expertise in printing money
  • Moscow – A veteran miner and expert in tunnel digging
  • Denver – Moscow’s impulsive and street-smart son
  • Helsinki & Oslo – Serbian twin brothers and muscle of the operation

Under The Professor’s guidance, the team trains for five months, learning the plan down to the smallest detail. The goal is not just to steal money but to print 2.4 billion euros inside the Royal Mint of Spain, a strategy that eliminates the risk of tracking stolen bills. The Professor also lays out strict rules: no personal relationships, no revealing real names, and no unnecessary violence.

The heist kicks off with the team infiltrating the Royal Mint, dressed in red jumpsuits and Salvador Dalí masks. The plan is executed flawlessly—hostages are taken, the exits are sealed, and police intervention is delayed. Among the hostages are:

  • Arturo Román, the Mint’s cunning but cowardly director
  • Mónica Gaztambide, Arturo’s secretary and secret lover, who is pregnant with his child
  • A group of schoolchildren on a field trip

Meanwhile, outside, Inspector Raquel Murillo is assigned to handle the crisis. She is a brilliant but troubled investigator dealing with a messy divorce and an abusive ex-husband. Unaware of his true identity, she unknowingly meets The Professor in a café, where he carefully manipulates their conversation to gain insight into the police response.

Inside the Mint, tensions begin to rise as Tokyo’s impulsive nature and Denver’s recklessness threaten to disrupt the carefully crafted plan. Meanwhile, Berlin establishes his authority over the hostages, ensuring order through psychological intimidation rather than immediate violence.

The episode ends on a high-stakes note as both the robbers and the police prepare for the long game ahead. The Professor remains one step ahead, orchestrating events from behind the scenes, while inside the Mint, the hostages begin looking for ways to fight back.

This gripping first episode sets the stage for an intense and strategic battle between the robbers and the police, filled with suspense, drama, and unexpected twists.
